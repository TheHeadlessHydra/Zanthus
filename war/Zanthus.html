ABOUT
Zanthus is a game currently in its alpha stages made by student Serj Kazar from the University of Waterloo. It is a tower defence game built using the three.js webGL API. For more information on how the game was built or the workflow required for importing custom meshes and animations into webGL, check out the projects section of my website. The game is currently best optimized for Chrome, but it will function in Firefox (albeit slowly). The game will not function in Internet Explorer.
CONTROLS
The object of the game is to last as long as possible. Waves of enemies will come at the tower and begin climbing. Once a single enemy reaches the top, its game over. On the right of the play area is a list of tower pieces you can purchase. Every time you purchase a piece, your tower grows in size. You can then use this tower piece to defend against the oncoming waves of enemies. Killing an enemy will reward you with some gold and allow you to purchase more tower pieces. To scroll around in the play area, hold the right mouse button and move up or down.
Different tower types and how to use them:

Fling Piece: The fling piece will kill any enemies currently at that position on the tower. To activate it, simply click on the tower itself. It has a cooldown of 15s represented by a purple glow. Once the glow dissapears, it is possible to click on it again.
